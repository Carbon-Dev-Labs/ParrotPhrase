About Parrot Phrase

Parrot Phrase (Parrot, a homophone of Para, and Phrase referring to the text.) is an inteligent text paraphrasing tool that uses python artifical inteligence librarys to modify your text to change the tone or writing style to match your parameters.

About The Developers
